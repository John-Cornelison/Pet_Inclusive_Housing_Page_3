Extremely Strong Relationship

  • Small and medium sized buildings are much more likely to not allow pets than large and very large buildings

Landlord Type Breakdown

  • Individual investors own a majority of units located in small properties while limited liability companies and partnerships own a majority of units in large properties

  • A property being managed by a property management company also plays an impact as to whether the property allows pets or not.

  • Properties are much more likely to not allow pets if they do not have a property management company.

Bills - California

CAA = California Apartment Association

  • A pet inclusive housing bill was introduced in California, but did not go through after objections from the CAA.
